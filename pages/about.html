ABOUT

Crafted by three computer science students, our prototype website not only replicates the functionality of popular ecommerce platforms but also serves as a practical learning ground for essential development practices. The team has integrated continuous integration techniques, ensuring that code changes are regularly and automatically tested to maintain a robust and error-free application. Leveraging tools like GitHub, the students have embraced collaborative development, enabling seamless version control, issue tracking, and collaborative contributions. This project not only showcases their technical proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript but also reflects a commitment to industry-relevant practices, fostering a comprehensive understanding of continuous integration and collaborative development tools.
